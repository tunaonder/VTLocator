Welcome to VTLocator!

A location-based web application for Virginia Tech students, faculty, and staff

  • Find the location of any building on campus, a picture, and a short description
  • Calculate distances and directions between two buildings
  • Locate parking lots and filter them by parking permissions
  • Find your lost items or help others get their belongings back

Built by: Sean Goodrich, Sait Tuna Onder, Jaemin Seo, Michael Steele, Sebastian Welsh
